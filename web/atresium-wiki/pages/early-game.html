The Manual Age

The begining

In Atresium you start with gathering rocks, stick and plants. The (flint)rocks will need to be further processed by rightclicking them on a hard surface. The plants will also need to be further processed into plant balls and in to plant string/fiber. Once you've done all of that you can get some flint tools(axe, pickaxe, etc.)
