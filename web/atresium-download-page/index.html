Atresium

Atresium a simple 2d sandbox/survival game based around real life technology and science.

The game is heavily inspired by games like minecraft and mindutry, but there is also allot of unique content in this game


This game is something for you if:

You like games like factorio, mindustry or minecraft.

You have allot of free time(like years).

You want to try and solve logistical puzzles

You are really intrested in real life science
